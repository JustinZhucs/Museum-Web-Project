The Palace Museum

It founded on October 10, 1925, is the largest museum in China, built on the foundation of the imperial palace (the Forbidden City) of the Ming and Qing dynasties, integrating architecture, collections, and the rich imperial history and culture contained therein.

Riverside Scene at Qingming Festival

One of the top ten famous Chinese paintings handed down from the past. The work, in the form of a long scroll and using the scattered perspective composition method, vividly records the urban appearance of Kaifeng, the capital of the Northern Song Dynasty in the 12th century of China, and the living conditions of people of all social classes at that time. It is a testimony to the prosperity of Bianjing.
